Final University Project

The animation for a project I made during my final year at University.

Project Overview

This project focused purely on the effects that the lighting of a cutscene has on a person's emotional responses to a cutscene, in order to gain a better understanding of the importance that lighting plays in the creation of a cutscene or cinematic.

To better understand the importance that lighting has on the emotional responses to a cutscene, this project aimed to create two cutscenes containing the exact same visual content, yet with completely different lighting setups. These cutscenes were then be shown to a number of participants to gauge their reaction to the cutscenes, and whether they change based on the differing lighting setups in each of them.
