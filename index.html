About Me

Hello! I'm George, an aspiring game developer based in The Netherlands. I studied Digital Games Development at the University of Brighton in the UK, and I'm set to graduate in July of 2024. During my studies I developed a passion for 3D modelling and animation, as well as a strong desire to further develop the programming skills that I've learned whilst undertaking my bachelors degree.

I'm a massive fan of video games and Tabletop RPGs such as Dungeons & Dragons and Masks, and a lover of metal music of all kinds. I've been doing martial arts since I was 12 years old, and have always been a huge fan of anything fantasy related: be it books, movies, music, or games.
